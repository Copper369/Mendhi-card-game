## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Open the game**: https://mendhikot-io.vercel.app
2. **Create a room** or **Join existing room**
3. **Wait for 4 players** (AI fills empty seats)
4. **Click "I'm Ready"** when all players join
5. **Choose trump suit** when it's your team's turn
6. **Play cards** during your turn
7. **Win by capturing all 4 tens!**

## 🎯 Game Rules Quick Reference

- **Deck**: 32 cards (7-Ace, all suits)
- **Players**: 4 (Team A vs Team B)
- **Objective**: Capture all 4 tens
- **Trump**: Alternates between teams
- **Win**: All 4 tens = win, split = draw
- **Match**: Best of 5 rounds
